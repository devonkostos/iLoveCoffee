Order Up
With over 38+ ways to make it and counting, you may have a hard time deciding how you'd like to drink your coffee. Most coffee lovers have a strong preference for the way they take their brews. Do you really love bacon? Want to incorporate it into your daily java? Woah, slow down there a second! Maybe you want to take a look below first and see some of the most popular ways people take their cuppa joe.
  • Espresso - The ultimate liquid energy, espresso is a small shot of caffeine at about 30ml.
  • Cappuccino - An eternal classic, cappuccinos are equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foamed milk (about 60ml each).
  • Latte - Beloved by many, the latte contains 60ml of espresso, 300ml steamed milk and 2ml foamed milk.
  • Mocha - Chocolate meets java in this deliciously sweet choice at 60ml of espresso, 60ml chocolate and 30ml steamed milk.
  • Americano - Probably the most popular choice here, the Americano is 30ml espresso and 60ml water.
  • Irish - Whiskey and cream are combined to create this supercalifrag ilisticexpialidocious choice sure to make you want to do a jig!
